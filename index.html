Locative

Automation par excellence

Imagine you're coming home, won't it be great to automatically switch your Lights On?
May be you'll leave your house while in a hurry and forget to switch your lights off.

No problem: Just configure your home-sweet-home as a geofence and let Locative do the rest! By calling a HTTP-URL (either via GET or POST request) you mal call your own WebService which then may trigger the desired operations.

 

Buy once, keep forever

Locative just calls a HTTP-URL and optionally displays the Server's HTTP-Response.

If your still in need of a reliable WebService to handle your requests, be sure to check out https://my.locative.io. Currently you may log your geofences, but we'll add other neat features pretty soon ;-)
